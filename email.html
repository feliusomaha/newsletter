Don’t forget our coffee shop!
 
 
Every cup you enjoy at Felius is doing double duty, helping to fund our rescue, medical care, and enrichment that give our cats a second chance. So whether you're sipping a latte in the company of a purring neighbor or grabbing your morning coffee on the go, you're helping us do what we love most — connecting people and cats in a way that changes both of their lives! Open 9AM - 7PM every day but Monday.
